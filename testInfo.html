Ultrasound

About the test

Ultrasounds are a non invasive imaging test that produces real-time images of the structure of internal soft tissues and organs through the use of high-frequency sound waves.

Prerequisites

  • Abdominal

  • 15-30 mins

  • Please don't drink or eat anything 12 hrs before your test
  • Drink at least 2 litres of water 1 hr before your test
  • Make sure to wear your mask when you arrive at the test centre
